Image URL Tips:
• Unsplash: Open photo → Right-click image → "Copy Image Address" → paste here
• Google Drive: Upload → Share → Copy link → Replace /file/d/ID/view with /uc?id=ID
• Cloudinary: Upload → copy the delivery URL directly
