Our Story

Gurez Born & Kashmir Proud

TripOrbit was founded by Umar Farooq, a native of Markoot village in Gurez Valley — one of Kashmir's most pristine and least-explored destinations. Growing up surrounded by the majestic Kishanganga River, the towering Habba Khatoon Peak and the warm Dard culture, Umar realized that most tourists were missing the real Gurez.

That's why he created TripOrbit — to bridge the gap between curious travellers and the authentic, untouched beauty of Gurez Valley. With deep local knowledge, trusted driver networks, handpicked accommodations and a commitment to transparency, TripOrbit has become the most trusted name for Gurez and Kashmir travel.

Today, TripOrbit serves travellers from across India and beyond — families, honeymooners, adventure seekers and solo explorers — all united by a desire to experience Kashmir and Gurez the right way.
