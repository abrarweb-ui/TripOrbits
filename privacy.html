Privacy Policy

How TripOrbit collects, uses and protects your information

Introduction

Welcome to TripOrbit. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website trip-orbit.com or contact us for travel services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name, phone number and email address when you submit an enquiry form
  • Travel preferences, travel dates and group details you voluntarily provide
  • Basic usage data through standard browser analytics (no personally identifiable data)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your travel enquiries and provide quotations

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only with trusted service partners (such as hotels and transport providers) strictly for fulfilling your booking.
